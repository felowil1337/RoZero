🩸 Crimson Weapons (Lv 70+)

Drop-only von Lv 70+ Field/Dungeon-Mobs. ATK-Formel: Base ATK + (RefineLv²) bis +15 + (Base Lv über 70 / 10) × 5. Beispiel: +15 Crimson @ Lv 175 = +275 ATK on top of Base.

17 Waffen-Varianten: Dagger (28705), Sword, 2H Sword, Mace (16040), Bow (18130), Katar (28007), Spear, 2H Axe, Rod, Knuckle, Revolver, Rifle, Shotgun, Gatling, Huuma, Whip, Instrument. Alle Lv 4, Lv 70+ req, alle 1-Slot.

🛒 Para Market (Lv 130+)

Nur Freitag/Samstag/Sonntag verfügbar. Zeny-only (kein Coin-System auf Zero).

  • Map: paramk 114/30 — Manager Parayo
  • Vendors: Nelsha (Weapons), Coco (Heavy Armors), Lapelt (Cloth/Leather)
  • Stock: Server-wide, Weekly Reset
  • Notable: Mysteltainn (ID 1138) — 1H Sword, ATK 170, Shadow, Lv 4, Lv 40+, DEX +3, Auto-Stone-Curse, +15% vs Ghost
